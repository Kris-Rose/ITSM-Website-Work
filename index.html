Home Business Outline About Us

Infinity Hot strives to create an empowering and uplifting feminine environment to improve the workout experience of those in our Highland Park community. We like to take it back to the roots, by offering genuine yoga, pilates, and more. Our instructor is certified in using techniques originating from Monchengladbach, Germany and is able to provide an authentic experience in our studios. We use innovative technology, such as infrared rooms heated at 105 degrees fahrenheit and top of the line equipment. Some of the benefits of the high temperature rooms in our studio include increased flexibility, improved cardiovascular health, and reduced stress. We strive to make exercise a sustainable habit within our studio by fostering a positive and encouraging environment. We not only offer one-of-kind classes, but also a loving community can be found inside the walls of Infinity Hot. Words of affirmations collected by employees, members of the community, and our very own Infinity crew can be found all around our studio to inspire our members to live a vibrant life.
